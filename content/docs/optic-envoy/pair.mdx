## Quick Pair

#### Open Optic desktop app [step]

Click "Settings" in the sidebar, click "Envoy" in the pop-up dialog, and then click "Start Pairing."
The interface may not change immediately; please wait a moment.

#### Check one-time code [step]

Then, the settings dialog would show a one-time code.

#### Open Optic Envoy [step]

Open Optic Envoy [here](https://web.opticlm.app) on any device's browser.

#### Enter one-time code and your name [step]

After opening the Optic Envoy webpage, enter your name and the one-time code that just appeared.

At this point, return to your computer,
where the Optic app will display a pop-up window asking if you want to allow (the name you entered)
to connect to Optic.

Please make sure the name matches the one you just entered, then agree.

#### Done [step]

Everything is ready! You can now use Optic on the web.
You'll see your familiar chat history and can continue working in your familiar workspace on your computer.
