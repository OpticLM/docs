## Introduction

Optic is an LLM client **designed for individuals**, aiming to integrate generative AI into our work and personal lives in ways we love.

Driven by a passion for generative AI and a commitment to responsibility,
OpticLM Studio is dedicated to delivering the best user experience.

We respect your privacy and do not collect any telemetry data.
We are unaffiliated with any LLM service provider,
allowing us to offer a framework that is optimally suited for each kind of LLM
while maintaining complete neutrality.
