---
title: Remote access
description: Access Optic anywhere.
---

With Optic Envoy, you can open Optic in a browser on any device and connect to the Optic app on your computer.

<Callout>Optic Envoy is only available for Optic Pro and Optic Max.</Callout>

You can:
- Work across multiple devices simultaneously
- Use it on your phone

You **don't need to**:
- Be on the same local network as your computer
- Create an account

Please read the Optic Envoy documentation to learn how to pair your devices.
